Solve for the value of $a$ in the following equations:

\vskip 20pt

Equation 1: $a - 4 = 10$

\vskip 10pt

Equation 2: $30 = a + 3$

\vskip 10pt

Equation 3: $-2a = 9$

\vskip 10pt

Equation 4: ${a \over 4} = 3.5$

\vskip 20pt

\underbar{file i01308}
%(END_QUESTION)





%(BEGIN_ANSWER)

Equation 1: $a = 14$

\vskip 10pt

Equation 2: $a = 27$

\vskip 10pt

Equation 3: $a = -4.5$

\vskip 10pt

Equation 4: $a = 14$

%(END_ANSWER)
