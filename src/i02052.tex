This formula describes the amount of {\it reactance} ($X_L$) exhibited by an inductor ($L$) at different frequencies ($f$):

$$X_L = 2 \pi f L$$

Algebraically manipulate this formula to solve for $L$:

\vskip 20pt

$L = $

\underbar{file i02052}
%(END_QUESTION)





%(BEGIN_ANSWER)

$$L = {X_L \over {2 \pi f}}$$
