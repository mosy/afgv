This is a technically diverse field!

\vskip 10pt

Impossible to completely prepare through schooling -- must continue to learn on your own.

\vskip 10pt

Effective technical reading is {\it the} key skill to have for self-directed learning.

\vskip 10pt

\noindent
Advice:
\item{} Build a personal, technical library
\item{} Mark up your books (or take notes)
\item{} ``Converse'' with the author
\item{} Write your thoughts during the learning process
\item{} Imagine explaining concepts to an intelligent child
\end{itemize}
